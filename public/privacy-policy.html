Privacy Policy for SAVVA

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Introduction

SAVVA is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and platform.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Wallet Information: Mnemonic phrases, private keys, and blockchain addresses (stored locally on your device)
  • User Profile: Username, bio, profile picture, and other profile information
  • Content: Posts, comments, and other content you create and share
  • Settings: App settings, feed preferences, and customization options

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used and interaction patterns
  • Blockchain Data: Transaction history and token balances (public data)

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: Enable wallet management, content posting, and social features
  • Security: Protect your account and detect fraudulent activity
  • Improvements: Analyze usage to improve app performance
  • Communication: Send important updates and security alerts
  • AI Features: Process requests when using AI assistant features

Data Storage and Security

Important Security Information:
  • All private keys are stored encrypted on your device only
  • We never have access to your mnemonic phrase or private keys
  • You are solely responsible for backing up your recovery phrase

Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption (AES-256, XSalsa20-Poly1305)
  • Optional biometric authentication (fingerprint/face unlock)
  • Secure HTTPS/WSS communications
  • No cloud backup of private keys

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data is public on blockchains
  • IPFS Network: Published content is distributed on IPFS
  • AI Services: Prompts sent to selected AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account (blockchain data is permanent)
  • Export your content
  • Opt-out of optional services
  • Correct your information

Blockchain Disclosures

  • All blockchain transactions are permanently public
  • Wallet addresses and transactions are visible on blockchain explorers
  • We cannot reverse transactions or recover lost keys
  • Never share your mnemonic phrase with anyone

Children's Privacy

SAVVA is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

AI Providers

  • OpenAI (GPT models) - Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude models) - Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini models) - Privacy Policy

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 90 days
  • Blockchain Data: Permanent and cannot be deleted
  • Server Logs: Retained for 90 days

Your Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including the right to know, delete, and opt-out. We do NOT sell personal information.

European Residents (GDPR)

EU residents have rights including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability under GDPR.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified through in-app notifications. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
