SAVVA Safety Standards & Child Protection Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

SAVVA has ZERO TOLERANCE for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of child safety and protection.

1. Age Restrictions

  • Minimum Age: Users must be at least 13 years of age to use SAVVA
  • Age Verification: Users must confirm their age during account creation
  • Parental Consent: Users aged 13-17 require parental or guardian consent in jurisdictions where required by law
  • Account Termination: Accounts of users found to be under 13 will be immediately terminated

2. Zero Tolerance Policy Against CSAE

SAVVA strictly prohibits any content, activity, or behavior related to child sexual abuse and exploitation, including but not limited to:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any form
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors
  • Sexualized content involving minors
  • Content that sexualizes, endangers, or exploits children
  • Sharing, distribution, or possession of CSAM
  • Any attempts to meet minors for illegal purposes

3. Content Moderation & Detection

3.1 Automated Systems

  • Hash Matching: Integration with NCMEC's hash database to detect known CSAM
  • AI Detection: Machine learning models to identify potential CSAE content
  • Pattern Recognition: Automated systems to detect grooming behavior patterns
  • Age Detection: AI-powered age estimation for content featuring individuals

3.2 Human Review

  • Trained content moderators review flagged content 24/7
  • All reports of suspected CSAE are prioritized for immediate review
  • Content moderators receive regular training on CSAE identification

4. User Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report suspicious content or behavior through:

  • In-app reporting button on all posts and profiles
  • Direct email to: abuse@alexna.ca
  • Emergency contact: support@alexna.ca

All reports are treated confidentially and reviewed within 24 hours. CSAE reports are escalated immediately.

5. Response Procedures

When CSAE is Detected or Reported:

  1. Immediate Action (Within 1 hour):
    • Content is removed immediately
    • Offending account is permanently banned
    • All associated accounts are investigated
  2. Law Enforcement Notification (Within 24 hours):
    • Report filed with National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
    • Report filed with Canadian Centre for Child Protection (C3P)
    • Local law enforcement notified as required
    • Full cooperation with law enforcement investigations
  3. Evidence Preservation:
    • All evidence is securely preserved for law enforcement
    • User information, IP addresses, and metadata retained
    • Blockchain transaction records maintained

6. Safety Features

  • Privacy Controls: Users can control who can contact them and view their content
  • Blocking: Users can block other users to prevent all interaction
  • Content Filtering: Age-appropriate content filters
  • Safe Search: Default safe search settings for all users
  • Direct Messaging Restrictions: Enhanced protections for minors in private messaging

7. Education & Prevention

  • In-app safety tips and resources for users and parents
  • Links to child safety organizations and hotlines
  • Regular safety awareness campaigns
  • Transparent community guidelines clearly outlining prohibited content

8. Compliance & Partnerships

SAVVA complies with and partners with leading child safety organizations:

  • NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children) - CyberTipline reporter
  • C3P (Canadian Centre for Child Protection) - Project Arachnid participant
  • ICMEC (International Centre for Missing & Exploited Children)
  • Tech Coalition - Industry safety standards adherence
  • Compliance with US COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • Compliance with EU GDPR child protection provisions

9. Regular Audits & Improvements

  • Quarterly safety audits by external child safety experts
  • Annual review and update of safety policies
  • Continuous improvement of detection technologies
  • Regular training for moderation teams
  • Transparency reports published annually

Emergency Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that endangers a child:

  • Immediate Danger: Contact local emergency services (911 in North America)
  • Report CSAM: CyberTip.ca (Canada) or CyberTipline.org (USA)
  • Report to SAVVA: abuse@alexna.ca

10. Enforcement

Violations of this policy result in:

  • Immediate and permanent account termination
  • IP address and device bans
  • Report to law enforcement
  • Report to NCMEC and C3P
  • No warnings or appeals for CSAE violations
