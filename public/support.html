Frequently Asked Questions

What is SAVVA?

SAVVA is a decentralized social platform built on blockchain technology. It allows users to publish content, interact with communities, and maintain ownership of their data through blockchain wallets.

How do I create an account?

SAVVA uses blockchain wallets instead of traditional accounts. When you first open the app, you can either create a new wallet (which generates a 12-word recovery phrase) or import an existing wallet using your recovery phrase.

What is a recovery phrase?

A recovery phrase (also called seed phrase or mnemonic) is a set of 12 words that gives you access to your wallet. Keep it safe and never share it with anyone. If you lose your recovery phrase, you will lose access to your wallet permanently.

Can I delete my content?

Due to the decentralized nature of blockchain technology, content published to the blockchain is permanent and cannot be deleted. Please consider this before publishing. You can delete your local account data through Settings → Account Management → Delete Account.

How do I report inappropriate content?

On any post, tap the "..." menu and select "Report". You can also block users to prevent seeing their content in your feeds. Blocked users can be managed in Settings → Account Management → Blocked Users.

What are network fees?

Some actions on SAVVA require blockchain transactions, which incur small network fees (called "gas fees"). These fees go to the blockchain network, not to SAVVA. Make sure you have enough native tokens (e.g., PLS on PulseChain) to cover transaction fees.

Safety Information

⚠️ Important Security Tips

  • Never share your recovery phrase with anyone, including SAVVA support.
  • SAVVA will never ask for your private keys or recovery phrase.
  • Beware of phishing - always verify you're on the official SAVVA app or website.
  • Back up your recovery phrase in a secure, offline location.
  • Report suspicious activity using the report feature or by contacting support.

Community Guidelines

To maintain a safe and welcoming environment, please follow these guidelines:

  • Be respectful and considerate of others.
  • Do not post illegal, harmful, or threatening content.
  • Do not harass, bully, or discriminate against others.
  • Do not share sexually explicit content involving minors.
  • Do not spam or engage in fraudulent activities.
  • Respect intellectual property rights.
  • Do not impersonate others or spread misinformation.

Violations may result in content removal and/or account restrictions. See our Terms of Service for more details.
